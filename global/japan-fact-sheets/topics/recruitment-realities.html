Recruitment feasibility in Japan

How to think about feasibility when targeting HCPs and patients in Japan.

Talk to us View sitemap

Key points

  • Feasibility can vary dramatically by specialty and region.
  • Rare disease segments require careful, respectful outreach.
  • Always align on minimum viable sample and contingency plans.

Practical planning checklist

  • Define inclusion/exclusion criteria clearly
  • Confirm screener language (Japanese nuance matters)
  • Decide early: online vs in-person constraints
