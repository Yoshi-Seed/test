Incentives & scheduling realities

A quick checklist for planning incentives and timelines in Japan.

Talk to us View sitemap

Key points

  • Plan incentives ethically and consistently across segments.
  • Allow buffer time for scheduling, rescheduling, and confirmation.
  • Be clear about participation requirements and burden.

Suggested questions for kickoff

  • What is the target profile and availability constraints?
  • Are there facility access requirements (hospital approvals, etc.)?
  • What are “no-go” hours and national holidays to avoid?
