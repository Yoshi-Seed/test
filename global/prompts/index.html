What we mean by “prompts”

Describe prompts as structured instructions that help standardize tasks (e.g., summaries, coding, consistency checks).

Where prompts help (examples)

  • Interview guide QA and consistency checks
  • Field notes structuring and tagging
  • Translation support (terminology consistency; not replacing human review)
  • Drafting executive summaries and slide skeletons

Human-in-the-loop by default

Make the quality stance explicit: prompts speed up work, but final decisions remain with experienced researchers.
